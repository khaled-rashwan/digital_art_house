Welcome to LibraPal

Read with Lexi & Lumi

LibraPal is an AI-powered virtual librarian application designed to help students discover books, stay motivated, and engage with reading in a fun and competitive way.

The world’s first virtual reading companion that brings books to life with personalized video recaps!

Main Features

GAMIFIED READING & COMPETITIONS
Smart Book Search & Recommendations

Students can ask the AI to find books and receive personalized recommendations.

Gamified Reading & Competitions

Students log books they’ve read, rate them, and mark books they plan to read.

Collaboration with National Libraries & Schools

More active readers can access exclusive library benefits.

AI BOOK RECAP VIDEO
Group tournament activities

Lexi or Lumi, the AI librarians, encourage and guide students through the process. The student’s avatar reads the summary in a 1–2 minute animated video. The system allows students to customize backgrounds and expressions. Videos can be shared on TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and other platforms.
